# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 2024**

At **MemoVox**, your privacy matters. We are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring you have full control over your information. This policy outlines what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under global privacy laws including GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA, and CPRA.

## 1. Data We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our AI-powered voice companion services:

### Account Information
*   **Email Address:** For authentication and account management.
*   **Name:** To personalize your experience.
*   **Account Creation Date:** For account administration.

### Voice Recordings & Content
*   **Audio Files:** The voice memos you record.
*   **Transcriptions:** Text converted from your audio.
*   **AI Analysis:** Summaries, insights, and chat history generated by our AI.

### Usage & Technical Data
*   **Usage Patterns:** How you interact with app features.
*   **Device Information:** Technical details about your device (for compatibility and troubleshooting).
*   **Performance Data:** Error logs and crash reports (only if you have enabled crash reporting).

---

## 2. How We Protect Your Data

Your security is our top priority.
*   **Encryption:** All data is encrypted in transit using TLS (Transport Layer Security).
*   **Secure Storage:** Your personal data and memos are stored securely.
*   **No Data Sales:** We **NEVER** sell your data to third parties.
*   **No Advertising:** We do not use your personal content for advertising purposes.

---

## 3. Third-Party Data Sharing

We partner with trusted services to deliver our features. Here is what they receive:

| Service | Purpose | Data Handled |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Groq AI** | Transcription & AI Intelligence | Processes voice audio (temporarily) and text for analysis. Data is **NOT** stored permanently by Groq. |
| **Supabase** | Database & Authentication | Stores account info and encrypted memo data. SOC 2 Type II certified. |
| **Expo** | App Infrastructure | Collects anonymous usage stats and crash reports (if enabled). |

---

## 4. Data Retention Policy

We keep your data only as long as you need it:

*   **Active Account:** Data is retained indefinitely while you use the app so you can access your history.
*   **Deleted Account:** When you delete your account, your data is **immediately purged** from our active systems.
*   **Backups:** Residual data in backups is removed within 30 days of account deletion.
*   **Legal Holds:** We retain data only if strictly required by law.

---

## 5. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You have full control over your data:

*   **Right to Access & Portability:** You can export all your data (voice memos, chat sessions, profile, and preferences) at any time as a JSON file directly from the app.
*   **Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten):** You can permanently delete your account and all associated data. This action is irreversible and includes all voice memos, transcriptions, and AI profiles.
*   **Right to Privacy Controls:** You can toggle settings for analytics and crash reporting within the app at any time.
